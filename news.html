COVID-19 NBA Update

As of March 11th, 2020, The NBA suspended their season until further notice due to the risk of a Utah Jazz player testing positive for COVID-19. As many fans of the NBA will remember this eveing they will be happy to hear that the league is beginning to explor there option on reopening the legue to finish the season. Many players, coaches and of course the fans are excited about this. LeBron James of the Los Angeles Lakers tweeted on Monday, "As soon as it's safe we would like to finish our season. I'm ready and our team is ready. Nobody should be canceling anything."

The NBA during the pandemic has come out with reports on possibly finishing the season at either a venue in Las Vages or at Disney World at the ESPN Wide World of Sports complex in Orlando, Flordia. Fans still will not be allowed to attend the games to keep the risk of players and coaches being exposed to the virus to a minimum. As a fan of the game many people will just be excited to have a sport to watch on TV. These are just possiblites at the moment nothing has been confirmed.
